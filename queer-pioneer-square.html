This tour contains the following scenes:

  • 1. Start: Barney's Loans, corner of S. Washington.
  • 2. 2nd Ave and James Street (arrow to the right of Barney's Loans, on the street with the long line of orange traffic barriers)
  • 3. 1st Ave and Yesler Way (arrow on the street in front of Butler's Garage)
  • 4. Occidental Park (arrow on 1st Ave)

Instructions:

In the tour window:

  • Launch full screen using the icon at top right (and press esc to exit).
  • There are 4 sites/scenes of 360° photos. Use your cursor to scroll around them to explore the landscape and to reveal popups or navigation arrows that might not be visible at first.
  • To move to another 360 photo, click the black arrows. To get back to the main scene (Conversation Cafe) look for the black arrow to take you back. Or reload to start over at your starting point.
  • In each scene, be sure to click each of the ⊕ icons to explore the information (text and media such as photographs, video) that describes the scene.
